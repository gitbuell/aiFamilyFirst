## APPENDIX: INFORMATION SOURCE REFERENCE GUIDE

### **Acceptable Sources for Each Data Category:**

| Data Category | Reliable Sources | Less Reliable | Action if Conflicting |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Medications** | 1) Prescription bottles (BEST) 2) Pharmacy records 3) Medical records | Patient recall only | Contact pharmacy; verify with patient |
| **Allergies** | 1) Prior documentation in medical record 2) Patient demonstrates reaction 3) Pharmacy allergy profile | Patient recall; family report | Mark as "patient-reported"; verify with documentation |
| **Family History** | 1) Direct report from relative 2) Relative's medical records 3) Death certificate for age/cause | Patient recall; family legend | Document source; note if hearsay |
| **Past Medical History** | 1) Medical records 2) Discharge summaries 3) Imaging reports | Patient recall | Request records for verification |
| **Vital Signs** | 1) Measured today in clinic (BEST) 2) Home monitoring records 3) Recent clinic measurements | Patient recall of past readings | Use today's measurements as baseline |
| **Medications/Dosages** | 1) Prescription bottle 2) Pharmacy records 3) Medical record | Patient recollection | Verify exact dose/frequency with pharmacy |
| **Lab Results** | 1) Original lab reports 2) Medical records with printed results 3) Electronic health records | Patient describing "doctor said..." | Request actual lab reports |
